Who We Are

Faith. Family.
Fitness.

Cretus Corpus Strength & Conditioning is a family-friendly and faith-based gym that places a huge emphasis on community. The people and coaches here strive to provide an encouraging and supportive environment where your body and soul can prosper.

"Whatever you do in word or deed, do all in the name of the Lord Jesus, giving thanks to God the Father through Him."

— Colossians 3:17

You may just be looking to stay healthy or find a starting point for your fitness journey, but we believe you'll find so much more than that here.

Drop-Ins & Visitors

Visiting the area or just want to try a class before committing? Drop-ins are welcome at Cretus Corpus Strength & Conditioning. Come in, meet the coaches and community, and get a workout in.
